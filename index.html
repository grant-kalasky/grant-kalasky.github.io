Hi, I'm Grant.

I am a technical product designer and product manager residing in Berkeley, CA. I'm passionate about the intersection of design, technology, and social systems.

Last summer, I interned at Blue Shield of California as a product designer. Before that, I interned at Dot Labs as a product design consultant and at Biomarker.io as a frontend developer and marketing intern.
